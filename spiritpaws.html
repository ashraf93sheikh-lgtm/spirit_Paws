About Our Shelter

At Paws Rescue Shelter, we are dedicated to creating a safe and compassionate world for stray and abandoned animals. Based in Tonk, Rajasthan, our NGO works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need, giving them a second chance at life. Every day, countless dogs, cats, and other small animals suffer due to neglect, injury, and lack of shelter. Our mission is to step in where help is needed most — providing medical care, food, shelter, and love to those who cannot speak for themselves. Our team consists of passionate volunteers, animal lovers, and local supporters who believe that every life matters. From rescuing injured animals on the streets to organizing adoption drives and awareness campaigns, we strive to build a community that treats animals with kindness and respect. We currently care for 20–25 animals, ensuring they receive proper nutrition, timely veterinary care, and a safe environment to recover and thrive. Beyond rescue, we focus on long-term solutions like sterilization drives and public education to reduce the number of homeless animals. 💛 Our Core Work Includes: Rescue and emergency response for injured and abandoned animals Shelter and daily care for rescued animals Medical treatment and vaccination Adoption and rehoming services Awareness campaigns about animal welfare Sterilization and population control initiatives We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can create a ripple effect of change. With your support, we aim to expand our efforts and reach more animals in need. 🐶 Together, we can give them the life they deserve.
